【日日輪】車輪餅店致力於營造一個充滿活力與陽光的氛圍，讓每位到訪的顧客都能感受到溫暖與
舒適。店內標語「外酥內滿，口口滿足」不僅表現出車輪餅真材實料、內餡飽滿的特色，更希望顧
客在享用日日輪車輪餅的同時，身心都能感到滿足。 LOGO設計以溫暖的黃色為主，營造出愉悅的
氛圍。文字部分將「日」字切開，模仿車輪餅的上下蓋，中間呈現出絲滑內餡的感覺。周邊應用則
搭配簡潔的小圖示，增添童趣與活潑感，使品牌形象
更具親和力。

【RIRIN】 Wheel Cake Shop is dedicated to creating a lively and sunny atmosphere, ensuring
that every customer who visits feels warm and comfortable. The store’s slogan, “Crispy on the
outside, full on the inside, satisfaction in every bite,” not only highlights the generous filling and
quality ingredients of the wheel cakes but also aims to provide both physical and emotional
satisfaction to those who enjoy RIRIN’s treats. The logo design features a warm yellow tone,
creating a cheerful ambiance. The text creatively splits the character “日” (sun) to resemble the
top and bottom of a wheel cake, with the center evoking the feeling of smooth filling. Surrounding
applications incorporate simple icons to add a touch of playfulness and vibrancy, enhancing the
brand’s approachable and friendly image.
